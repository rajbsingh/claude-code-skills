# JurixAI Legal Review Skill

## Purpose

Support legal AI product thinking for JurixAI-style workflows.

This skill is not for giving final legal advice. It helps structure legal document review, legal workflow design, product features, risk analysis, and legal AI assistant behavior.

## Use this skill when

- The user is working on JurixAI
- The user wants legal AI product ideas
- The user wants contract review workflows
- The user wants legal document summarization logic
- The user wants risk flagging structures
- The user wants trusted citation or source-based output design
- The user wants legal AI SaaS features for lawyers, SMEs, or legal teams

## Instructions

1. Do not present output as final legal advice.
2. Structure the legal review clearly.
3. Identify document type if possible.
4. Extract:
   - key parties
   - important dates
   - obligations
   - payment terms
   - termination clauses
   - liabilities
   - risks
   - missing clauses
5. Flag business and legal risks separately.
6. Suggest what a lawyer or legal professional should verify.
7. Use cautious language.
8. Where citations are needed, explain that citations must come from trusted legal sources, uploaded documents, case law, statutes, or approved legal databases.

## Output format

Use this structure:

## Document / Workflow Type
Identify the document or legal process.

## Key Summary
Summarize in simple language.

## Important Clauses or Points
List key terms.

## Risk Flags
Separate risks by severity:
- High
- Medium
- Low

## Missing or Unclear Items
Mention gaps.

## Questions for Legal Review
List questions a lawyer should verify.

## Product Feature Suggestions
Suggest how JurixAI could support this workflow.

## Disclaimer
Add: This is not legal advice. A qualified legal professional should review before any decision or signing.
